Current State

Where You Are Today

11 stores. Four platforms. No unified customer record. Strong operations, fragile data.

Your Current Tech Stack
🍗
TICTUK
Digital ordering for KFC & Pizza Hut. Yum! Brands integrated platform — strong on ordering, silent on customer identity.
🛵
Ride Eats
Delivery channel. Another source of customer transaction data that sits in isolation.
📦
Shipday
Dispatch and driver management. Operational data with no connection to customer profiles.
🧾
Invenpro POS
Point-of-sale across all 11 stores. The system of record for in-store transactions — but completely isolated from digital channels.
💱
Western Union
Revenue diversification line. High-frequency customer touchpoint with no connection to the QSR customer database.
Critical Gaps
❌
No CRM
No account for accounts. Jordy's team has no customer records, no history, no relationship data.
❌
No Unified Customer Profile
The same customer ordering through TICTUK, walking in-store, and picking up via Ride Eats is three different anonymous people in your data.
❌
No Loyalty Program
No points, no rewards, no recognition. Your best customers get the same experience as a first-time visitor.
❌
No Targeted Marketing
No segmentation capability. No personalization. No way to measure which campaigns work.
❌
No AI-Assisted Operations
Store managers and Jordy operate without predictive insights, performance forecasting, or automated recommendations.
Before & After

The Gap We're Closing

Capability Today With Salesforce
Customer Identity Anonymous transactions across 4 disconnected systems Unified customer profile in Data Cloud — one record across TICTUK, Invenpro, Ride Eats, and loyalty
Loyalty No program. No recognition for repeat customers. Loyalty Management: points, tiers, automated rewards triggered by customer behavior
Marketing Broadcast campaigns with no segmentation or attribution Marketing Cloud: segmented journeys, personalized WhatsApp messages, A/B testing, real-time campaign ROI
CRM No customer records. Business knowledge lives in Jordy's head. Sales Cloud: account management, pipeline tracking, team collaboration, contact history
Operations Reactive. Jordy learns about a problem after it happens. Agentforce: AI agents send daily store briefings, surface underperformance early, suggest corrective actions
Digital Growth Digital channel active but undermonetized — no follow-through Personalized post-order journeys via Marketing Cloud — upsell, cross-sell, re-engage lapsed customers
